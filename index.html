About Us

Established by Greg Hall, Greg Hall Enterprises is a distinguished business specializing in the staining and painting of precast concrete. This enterprise takes pride in its unique ownership structure, being led by a veteran and a woman. With a rich history spanning over 35 years, we have honed our expertise in the industrial painting and staining of precast concrete structures, including bridges, embankments, parking garages, and sound walls. Our services have significantly impacted the Ohio Valley area, marking us as leaders in our field. Currently headquartered in Kentucky, our operations extend across the Midwest and East Coast, serving a diverse range of clients with our unparalleled precast concrete staining and painting services.
